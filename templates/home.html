    Medical Visual Question Answering

    By combining medical expertise with artificial intelligence, AI systems like MediSight can answer medical questions more efficiently, revolutionizing the way users access and understand medical knowledge

    MediSight works by processing and analyzing images and text to provide accurate and comprehensive medical information. When a user inputs an image and a text question about medical topics, the AI model leverages its pre-trained knowledge and advanced algorithms to understand the context and provide a relevant answer. This process involves several steps:

    • 1

      The AI model encodes the image and text question into a format that can be processed and understood by the system

    • 2

      The model uses its pre-trained knowledge to identify relevant information from the encoded image and text

    • 3

      The system communicates its findings using multiple modalities, providing both text answers and dynamically annotated images

    BLIP Model

    MediSight's driven by BLIP Model

    The BLIP model was proposed in BLIP: Bootstrapping Language-Image Pre-training for Unified Vision-Language Understanding and Generation by Junnan Li, Dongxu Li, Caiming Xiong, Steven Hoi.

    The BLIP (Bootstrapping Language-Image Pre-training) model is a cutting-edge pre-training framework for unified vision-language understanding and generation. It achieves state-of-the-art results on various vision-language tasks, including visual question answering, image-text retrieval, and image captioning. BLIP is jointly pre-trained with three vision-language objectives: image-text contrastive learning, image-text matching, and image-text generation, making it a versatile and powerful model for multimodal tasks.
